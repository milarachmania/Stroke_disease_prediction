## Introduction
Stroke is a condition that occurs when the blood supply to the brain is interrupted or reduced due to an ischemic stroke or a blood vessel rupture (hemorrhagic stroke). According to the World Health Organisation (WHO) stroke is the 2nd leading cause of death globally, responsible for approximately 11% of total deaths.
